What is Robotics?

  • Robotics is a branch of engineering and computer science.
  • It deals with the design, construction, operation, and programming of robots.
  • Robots are machines that can perform tasks automatically.

Components of a Robot

  • Sensors – detect surroundings.
  • Controller – the robot's brain.
  • Controller – the robot's brain.
  • Power Supply – provides energy.

Robotics is one of the most exciting technologies of the modern world. From helping at home to exploring space, robots are making life easier and opening new possibilities for the future.

Did You Understant?

1. Informative / Expository Style Robotics is a branch of technology that deals with the design, construction, and operation of robots. These machines can be programmed to perform tasks ranging from simple household chores to complex industrial operations. Modern robotics combines mechanical engineering, electronics, and computer science to create systems that can work with precision, speed, and efficiency. From manufacturing cars to assisting in surgeries, robots are transforming the way humans live and work.

2. Descriptive Imaginative Style Imagine a world where tireless helpers work alongside humans, lifting heavy loads, exploring distant planets, and even caring for the sick. This is the world of robotics—a field where metal meets intelligence. With sensors as their eyes and algorithms as their brains, robots can navigate spaces, make decisions, and carry out tasks with remarkable accuracy. They are not just machines; they are the bridge between human creativity and technological possibility.
